National Research Foundation of Korea, 2020-2026

Research center for metamorphing mechanical systems

A long-term research program on mechanical systems that transform shape and function through responsive materials, soft structures, and coupled transport phenomena.

Focus Areas

  • Heat- and moisture-transport-driven material deformation.
  • Fluid transport in high-aspect-ratio nanopatterns and porous media.
  • Hydrogel synthesis, adhesion, and responsive deformation.
