Mechanics of water-powered soft actuation

Soft actuation Hydrogel Water transport

An invited review on the mechanics of water-powered soft actuation, covering transport-driven deformation, swelling, and responsive material systems.

Research Notes

  • Surveys mechanisms where water transport powers soft material motion.
  • Connects swelling, capillarity, poroelasticity, and hydrogel mechanics.
  • Authors include S. Jung*, C. Kim*, T. Kim, S. Park, and H.-Y. Kim†.
  • * Equal contribution, † Corresponding author.
