Soft Robotics, 2023

Sequential multi-modal morphing of single-input pneu-nets

Soft Robotics Pneumatic actuation Sequential morphing

A soft robotics paper on single-input pneumatic networks that can produce sequential deformation modes through structural and stiffness design.

Research Notes

  • Demonstrates multi-modal morphing from a single pneumatic input.
  • Highlights design rules for sequential bending and torsional deformation.
  • Authors include H. Jeong, C. Kim, A. Lee, and H.-Y. Kim.
