Viola seed pod architecture shapes sequential, force-augmented pinching

Plant morphing Poroelasticity Humidity response

A study on the architecture and mechanics of Viola seed pods, focusing on how humidity-responsive plant structures generate sequential, force-augmented pinching.

Research Notes

  • Explores plant-inspired morphing mechanisms and sequential actuation.
  • Connects material transport, deformation, and mechanical amplification.
  • Authors include C. Kim, J. Won, D. Kim, S. Jung, H.-Y. Kim, and Y. Hyun.
